Welcome to Flora

Flora is a locally-owned houseplant shop with a brick & mortar location in Huntsville, AL.
We specialize in tropical, desert, and rare houseplants.



Need Help With Your Plants?

Sometimes you just need help with plants, and we're here for you! Whether you're wanting to add new plants or need help diagnosing problems with your existing ones, our team is trained to answer your questions and assist you on your plant journey.

Have questions about repotting? Which plants will work best with your lighting? How to eradicate spider mites? From Plant Guru to Plant Doctor, we're here to help!

We offer virtual plant consultations via Zoom/Facetime as well as in-person consultations for our local folks in Huntsville.
